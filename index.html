About

With a Bachelor's degree in Physics and a passion for storytelling and technology, I strive to make visual effects to a standard that leaves a positive impact on the audience.

Currently a VFX student at the National Film and Television School, I specialize in compositing, including rotoscoping, plate preparation, marker removal and cleanup, tracking and 3D compositing. Have also experienced all aspects of the VFX pipeline, from previz and modelling, to on-set and post-production, such as HDRIs, photogrammetry, set survey, and matchmove.

Experienced in using Nuke, as well as the fundamentals of Maya, Unreal Engine, Photoshop and PTGui. Also have knowledge of Python, HTML and CSS, the former of which has been utilized to produce basic Nuke scripts to optimize workflow.
